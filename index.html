Locations

Sharing culture, celebrating accomplishments, and forming bonds while working side-by-side with the locals is a critical part of the development process. Regardless of when or where, GIVE will always involve you in unique, innovative projects that empower our host communities, using cutting-edge, eco-friendly techniques.

Whether you're releasing baby sea turtles in Nicaragua, protecting Moon Bears from poaching in Laos, diving with dolphins in Zanzibar, or playing with elephants in Thailand, you can rest assured that all projects and activities are designed to protect these amazing species and ensure that they're still around for future generations to enjoy.

Nicaragua

Nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea, Nica is known as "The Land of Lakes and Volcanoes." The coasts vary tremendously in culture, topography, and activities.
